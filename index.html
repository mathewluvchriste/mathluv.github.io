RESEARCH PROJECT

AI-Based Smart Water Quality Monitoring and Autonomous Protection System


Research project integrating Artificial Intelligence, Differential Evolution optimization, EPANET hydraulic simulation, and digital twin technology for intelligent smart water infrastructure monitoring.


The developed framework continuously monitors chlorine concentration, turbidity, pressure, and temperature while performing AI-based contamination prediction and autonomous emergency response control.


The system can automatically isolate contaminated network zones using smart valve control and provide intelligent operational recommendations for smart city water infrastructures.


Key Features

  • Real-time water quality monitoring
  • AI contamination prediction engine
  • Differential Evolution optimization
  • EPANET hydraulic network simulation
  • Autonomous smart valve control
  • Emergency contamination isolation
  • Digital Twin dashboard visualization
  • Smart city infrastructure integration
